Mini Wiki FAQ

What is The Forge Mini Wiki?
The Forge Mini Wiki is a compact reference for Roblox The Forge, listing ore multipliers and traits alongside weapon, armor, pickaxe, rune, and race stats so you can compare crafting data quickly.
Where does the mini wiki data come from?
The mini wiki uses a community wiki snapshot and is intended as a quick reference. For the latest updates, compare with in-game changes.
